Who?

My name is Athena Jasso and I’m 16 years old. For my whole life I’ve liked drawing, although it became more prominent when I was 10 years old. In the past I made drawings whenever I had nightmares but now I usually do it when I’m bored.

“People would consider my drawings odd and weird, but in my opinion they are original.”
-Athena Jasso
